We Are ROOST

The Roost is a Non-Profit Organization located at 2020 E 7th St, Los Angeles, CA. 90021. We aim to build relationships and create social change through gastronomy while supporting local artists, farmers, and businesses. Our establishment serves as a community kitchen, a revolving gallery featuring the work of local artists, and operates entirely on volunteer work.
